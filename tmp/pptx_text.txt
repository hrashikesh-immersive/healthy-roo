The Process
The school collaborates with Healthyroo, choosing services like health checkups, wellness programs, infrastructure setup, or workshops. These are scheduled to avoid disrupting the academic calendar.
On assessment day, Healthyroo's medical team sets up health stations on campus to screen children for physical, dental, vision, ENT, and growth parameters during school hours.
Healthyroo promotes year-round health in schools through monthly CARE Boxes, student Wellness Clubs, quarterly wellness themes, and teacher training, ensuring wellness is prioritized every day, not just during checkups.
Parents receive detailed digital health reports for their child within 24 hours through the MyHealth app, featuring color-coded results, expert recommendations, and alerts for any concerns needing follow-up.
