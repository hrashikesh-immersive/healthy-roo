# Redesigning the About Us Page into a Founder Portfolio

The `About Us` page has been successfully redesigned into a stunning, long-format portfolio focusing on the founder, Smit Tanksale. 

### Implementation Details
- **Stitch MCP Generation**: Created a dedicated design project and generated the layout and structure tailored specifically for a high-end personal portfolio page, incorporating the requested text from the `founder.pdf`.
- **Aesthetics & Theme**: Maintained the original HealthyRoo color palette, emphasizing the vibrant primary red (`#EE1D26`) against clean white and gray backgrounds for a crisp, high-impact look.
- **Images**: Integrated the provided `image.png` into the page. To ensure visual variety, the single image is utilized across different sections (like the Hero and Athletics sections) with distinct cropping, styling, and masonry layouts to emulate a full portfolio feel.
- **Dynamic Interactions**: Enhanced the page with Framer Motion, adding polished scroll animations that trigger as users navigate down the long-format page.

### Sections Included
1. **Hero Area**: Bold "SMIT TANKSALE" typography with his background as an engineer from the University of Melbourne.
2. **Engineering & AI**: Highlighted his role as the architect of HealthyRoo, with a beautiful grid detailing the Hybrid Architecture and "Curiosity Engines."
3. **Athletics & Leadership**: A visual masonry grid displaying his sporting background, connecting his disciplined athletic life (cricket, futsal, football) to his leadership style.
4. **The Quote**: The page finishes with a full-width, deep red section featuring the expansive quote about his life as an athlete being the blueprint for his entrepreneurial journey.

### Verification
You can view the new page by checking the running dev server in your browser. The layout is fully responsive and optimized for both desktop and mobile viewing.
